Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about shipping, returns, payments, and products.

Shipping

How long does delivery take?

Typically 2–4 business days for inner-city areas and 3–7 days for other regions. During peak seasons, it may take 1–2 extra days.

How is the shipping fee calculated?

The fee is shown at checkout based on province/city and order weight. Orders from 999k are eligible for free domestic shipping.

Can I change the address after placing an order?

Yes, if the parcel hasn’t been handed over to the carrier. Please contact Customer Support to update it as soon as possible.

Returns

Return window

You may return or exchange within 7 days of receipt. Items must have original tags, be unused, and remain in original condition.

Refund policy

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 3–7 business days after our warehouse confirms eligibility.

Payments & Products

Do you support Cash on Delivery (COD)?

Yes, COD is available for most domestic addresses. Please check any applicable COD fee at checkout.

Are the colors on photos the same as in real life?

Images are calibrated for accuracy, but slight variations may occur due to lighting conditions and device displays.

Still need help?

Contact our team during business hours for assistance.
